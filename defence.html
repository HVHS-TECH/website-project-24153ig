Goal Keeper

Where position can go on court

Drill

Rapid fire saves drill

How to do this drill

  • The GK starts in the shooting circle
  • A feeder stands outside the circle with multiple balls
  • The feeder quickly shoots or passes balls rapidly towards the goal from different angles
  • The GK must attempt to block or save each shot
  • After each save or attempt, the feeder immediately sends the next ball without delay
  • The drill keeps going until the GK has made 20 blocks or intercepts

This drill is really good for GK because it improves reaction time and hand-eye coordination. It also helps them stay focused and practice making quick saves.

Goal Defence

1 vs 1 circle defence drill

How to do this drill

  • One attacker and the GD start inside the shooting circle
  • Two feeders stand outside the circle and pass the ball to each other
  • The GD stays close to the attacker and marks them
  • As the ball gets passed between the feeders, the GD moves side to side to stay between the attacker and the ball
  • The attacker tries to get free and receive the pass inside the circle
  • The GD tries to block the pass
